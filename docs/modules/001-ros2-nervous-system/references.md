# Reference Management System for APA Citations

## Purpose

This document outlines the proper format and management of references for the ROS 2 Nervous System module, ensuring all sources meet APA citation standards as required by the project constitution.

## APA Citation Format Guidelines

### Books
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle*. Publisher Name.

Example: Quigley, M., Gerkey, B., & Smart, W. (2015). *Programming robots with ROS: A practical introduction to the development of autonomous robots*. O'Reilly Media.

### Journal Articles
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

Example: Smith, J. D., & Jones, M. P. (2018). Advances in robotic middleware systems. *Journal of Robotics*, 15(3), 123-145. https://doi.org/10.1155/2018/1234567

### Online Documentation
Author/Organization. (Year, Month Date). Title of document. *Website Name*. URL

Example: Open Robotics. (2023, June 15). ROS 2 documentation. *ROS Documentation*. https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/

### Conference Papers
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year, Month). Title of paper. In *Proceedings of the Conference Name* (pp. page range). Publisher. DOI or URL

Example: Nahidi, A., Sharma, S. K., & Xiao, J. (2018). ROS 2: Towards trustworthy robotic computing. In *2018 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS)* (pp. 7294-7301). IEEE. https://doi.org/10.1109/IROS.2018.8593545
